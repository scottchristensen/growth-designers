# Summary: Synthetic User Feedback Scale

## Key Benefits
- Provides a quick spot check.
- Is 100 times faster than gathering live feedback from personas.
- Helps catch small details that might otherwise be missed or would require extensive in-person user feedback.
- Useful for quick validation and spot checking one's thinking.
- Surprisingly useful for putting oneself in the headspace of different personas.

## Use Cases
- Spot-checking during a pricing revamp.
- Validating a new website.
- Identifying user skepticism, such as when pricing is not visible by default.
- Discovering missing listed discounts for larger organizations.
- Generating stream of consciousness feedback and summaries from predefined personas for a product (e.g., Cloudfluent).
