# 7 Cheap(er) User Testing Tools for Lean Growth Teams

**Source:** [growth-designers.beehiiv.com](https://growth-designers.beehiiv.com/p/7-cheaper-user-testing-tools-for-lean-growth-teams)
**Author:** Molly Norris Walker
**Published:** March 03, 2026

---

The user testing market is consolidating, with major acquisitions driving prices to $20k-$60k+ annually. This article highlights seven more affordable alternatives for lean growth teams seeking quality research without enterprise pricing.

## Main Tools Reviewed

### 1. Userlytics
- **Starting Cost:** ~$34+ per participant (pay-as-you-go) or $699+ annually
- **Free Plan:** Trial credits available; 2-for-1 promotions on sessions
- **Features:** Unmoderated testing, video recordings, global recruitment, tree tests, card sorts, transcription analysis
- **Best For:** Structured usability testing without enterprise lock-in
- **Community Feedback:** "They have an enormous and diverse international panel and it's not expensive"

### 2. Great Question
- **Starting Cost:** ~$99/month for starter plans
- **Free Plan:** Yes, with limitations on seats and responses
- **Features:** Surveys, preference testing, research CRM, recruitment integrations
- **Best For:** Fast validation and messaging tests

### 3. Lyssna
- **Starting Cost:** ~$83/month
- **Free Plan:** Yes, with limited tests and responses
- **Features:** Five-second tests, first-click tests, preference tests, card sorting, tree testing
- **Best For:** Rapid directional UI feedback
- **Community Note:** Some members reported cost escalation over time

### 4. Maze
- **Starting Cost:** Scales by seats and responses
- **Free Plan:** Yes, with limited projects and monthly responses
- **Features:** Figma integration, prototype testing, surveys, built-in analytics
- **Best For:** Product teams validating designs directly from files
- **Community Feedback:** "Not really expensive and easy to use"

## Honorable Mentions

- **Userbrain:** Straightforward subscription-based testing with task flows and recordings
- **UXtweak:** All-in-one research suite with tree testing, card sorting, and session recordings
- **Ballpark:** Polished platform acquiring UserTesting assets; opaque pricing suggests enterprise focus

## Key Takeaway

As consolidation continues pressuring prices upward, growth teams increasingly seek alternatives. The article emphasizes protecting momentum through creative recruiting and exploring emerging tools. The author encourages community members to share experiences in the GrowthDesigners.co Slack.
